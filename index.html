About SWIM@KSoM 2025

Welcome to SWIM@KSoM 2025, an intensive instructional school dedicated to fostering a deeper understanding of foundational mathematics. Hosted at the serene Kannur University (Thavakkara Campus) from May 05 to May 30, 2025, this workshop offers a rigorous immersion into core mathematical disciplines. Over four weeks, participants will engage with approximately 40 comprehensive lectures spanning Analysis, Algebra, and Linear Algebra. These lectures are thoughtfully complemented by dedicated problem-solving sessions led by experienced teaching assistants, providing invaluable opportunities for hands-on learning and conceptual reinforcement. SWIM@KSoM is specifically designed for highly motivated undergraduate students entering their third year, offering them a unique platform to consolidate their knowledge and prepare for advanced studies. The program proudly features lectures delivered by a distinguished faculty of experienced and renowned mathematicians, bringing a wealth of expertise and insight to the classroom.
